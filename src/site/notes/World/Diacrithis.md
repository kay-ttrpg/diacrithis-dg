Realm where the [[Diacrithis Chronicles\|Diacrithis Chronicles]] take place.
Separated in two by the [[World/Onyx Divider\|Onyx Divider]]. 
To the west of the realm is the [[Ivory Alliance\|Ivory Alliance]], to the east, the [[Wild Lands\|Wild Lands]].
