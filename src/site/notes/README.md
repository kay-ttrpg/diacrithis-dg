Welcome to [[Diacrithis Chronicles\|Diacrithis Chronicles]]!

[[Diacrithis Chronicles\|Diacrithis Chronicles]] is the name of my collective D&D 5e 2024 campaigns.
In the year [[321\|321 CE]], a [[The Fault\|crack]] appeared on the [[World/Onyx Divider\|Onyx Divider]], a magically erected wall that kept the [[Ivory Alliance\|West]] isolated from the [[Wild Lands\|East]], a wild land touched by a [[Great Calamity\|strange, unpredictable magic]] coming from the [[Far East\|far east of the continent]].
The apparition of this crack through off-balance the flimsy peace standing between the six nations of the [[Ivory Alliance\|Ivory Alliance]]. 

What will come of this event?
What kind of heroes will it shape?
How will the civilized people adapt to this new paradigm?

*Only the players can tell...*
