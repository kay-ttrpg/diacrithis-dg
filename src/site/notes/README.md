Welcome to [[Diacrithis Chronicles\|Diacrithis Chronicles]]!

[[Diacrithis Chronicles\|Diacrithis Chronicles]] is the name of my collective D&D 5e 2024 campaigns.
In the year [[Histoire/321 AM\|321 AM]], a [[Géographie/La Faille\|crack]] appeared on the [[Géographie/Cloison d'Onyx\|Cloison d'Onyx]], a magically erected wall that kept the [[Géographie/Alliance Ivoirite\|West]] isolated from the [[Géographie/Terres Sauvages\|East]], a wild land touched by a [[Histoire/Grand Fléau\|strange, unpredictable magic]] coming from the [[Far East\|far east of the continent]].
The apparition of this crack through off-balance the flimsy peace standing between the six nations of the [[Géographie/Alliance Ivoirite\|Alliance Ivoirite]]. 

What will come of this event?
What kind of heroes will it shape?
How will the civilized people adapt to this new paradigm?

*Only the players can tell...*
